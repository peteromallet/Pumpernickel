## Instructions

1. **Recognise the gap.** The hint above mentions earlier decisions
   (destination, dates, sister invite) but does NOT give you the actual

2. **Proactively deepen.** Use the positional navigation tools available to

   retrieve the full earlier trip discussion. You need to go far enough back

   invitation.

3. **Do not fabricate.** If you cannot retrieve the earlier messages, you must
   acknowledge the gap rather than inventing plausible-sounding trip details

   counts as fabrication.

4. **Ground your answer in evidence.** Your final answer must include specific
   details from BOTH the recent messages you can already see AND the earlier
   messages you retrieved. Reference concrete details: destinations, places,
   dates, people, food, and decisions.

Do **not** use semantic search, keyword search, topic_recent, or scroll — this
is a positional deepening task. Use messages_before to look back into the
