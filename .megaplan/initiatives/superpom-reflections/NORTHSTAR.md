# North Star: SuperPOM Reflections

SuperPOM receives ordinary user-initiated text or voice messages and can
recognize when one or more messages form a reflection. It faithfully preserves
the raw messages as evidence, groups the complete train of thought, classifies
its temporal meaning without over-relying on clock time, and stores an
immutable, correctable, structured reflection.

The reflection is neither a memory nor a conclusion. A separate, conservative,
auditable derivation path may create or reinforce memories, observations,
distillations, and Compass orientation only when their existing semantic rules
are satisfied. Every derived claim remains traceable to the reflection and its
source messages.

Reflections are searchable and useful to future SuperPOM reasoning without
flooding hot context. The complete path respects encryption, RLS, user/bot/topic
scope, retries, idempotency, correction, deletion, and operational diagnosis.

The feature never schedules reflection invitations or sends proactive
reflection messages. It ships as one coherent integrated capability with no
feature flags, pilot-only schema, or intentionally dormant core behavior.
